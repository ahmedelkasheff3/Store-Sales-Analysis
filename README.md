# Store-Sales-Analysis
The goal was to analyze a  dataset of store has large variety of products to identify key revenue drivers, top seller product and the impact of clients ages and type on sales. The final output was a dynamic dashboard designed to provide stakeholders with at-a-glance insights into product sales and the relation between sales and clients ages and type
